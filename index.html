FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How long does it take to build my site?

You'll see a design draft within 48 hours of our call. The full build typically takes 1–2 weeks depending on revisions and how quickly you provide content.

Do I need to provide my own content?

Ideally yes — you know your business best. But we can help with copywriting and source professional photography if needed.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Absolutely. Every site on the Growth and Store plans includes a CMS so you can update text, images, and prices without touching any code.

What about hosting and my domain name?

We guide you through registering a domain and set up fast, reliable hosting. Hosting costs around €10–20/month, handled separately from our build fee.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. We offer optional monthly maintenance plans from €99/month covering updates, security, backups, and small content changes.

What if I need something custom?

Just reach out and describe what you need. We take on bespoke projects too — we'll put together a custom quote.
